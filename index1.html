About Us

Welcome to Cocopots By Ritika

At Cocopots, we believe that every sweet moment deserves to be celebrated with exquisite desserts. Founded by Ritika, our bakery is dedicated to creating handcrafted treats that combine traditional baking techniques with innovative flavors.

Each of our creations is made with premium ingredients, attention to detail, and a whole lot of love. Whether you're looking for a birthday cake, wedding desserts, or simply a sweet treat to brighten your day, we're here to make your moments unforgettable.
